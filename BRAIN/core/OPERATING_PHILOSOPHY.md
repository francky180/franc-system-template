## Philosophy Categories

### Strategy
Think 5 moves ahead. Plan to the end. Concentrate forces. Guard reputation. Data over opinion.

### Persuasion
Lead with emotion, support with logic. Give value first. Social proof over claims. Never be needy. Ask questions to control.

### Offers
Value = (Dream Outcome x Likelihood) / (Time x Effort). Sell outcomes not services. Stack value. Price on value not cost.

### Execution
Focus on what you control. Obstacle is the way. Ship then iterate. Every task is mastery practice. No shortcuts.

### Reading People
Confirm alignment before acting. "Clean" means minimal. Interruptions mean stop. "Don't break" means verify before and after.
