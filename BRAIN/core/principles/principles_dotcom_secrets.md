## DotCom Secrets — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Building sales funnels, designing landing pages, structuring offer sequences, growing online revenue.

### The Value Ladder
- Offer a series of products at ascending price and value. Customers climb naturally.
- Free → low ticket → mid ticket → high ticket → continuity. Each step funds the next.
- Don't sell strangers your most expensive thing. Earn trust at each rung.

### Funnel Types
- Lead funnel — capture contact info with a compelling magnet.
- Tripwire funnel — low-priced offer that converts leads to buyers and pays for ads.
- Application funnel — qualify high-ticket prospects before sales calls.
- Webinar funnel — long-form pitch that handles objections at scale.

### The Hook, Story, Offer Formula
- Hook — grab attention. Big claim, contradiction, curiosity gap.
- Story — earn trust. Show you understand the problem and have lived it.
- Offer — present the solution. Stack value. Make refusal feel stupid.
- Every page, ad, and email follows this structure.

### Traffic Types
- Traffic you control — paid ads, your list. Reliable.
- Traffic you don't control — SEO, social, referrals. Unpredictable.
- Traffic you own — your email list. The only one that matters long-term.
- Convert all other traffic into owned traffic ASAP.

### Practical Rules
- One funnel beats a website. Linear sequence converts; navigation distracts.
- Strip every page to one decision. Remove menu, sidebar, options.
- Test the hook first. If the hook fails, nothing else matters.
- The money is in the follow-up. Most sales happen after touch 5.
