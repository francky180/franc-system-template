## I Will Teach You to Be Rich — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Personal finance setup, debt elimination, automating savings, designing a rich life.

### Automate Everything
- Willpower fails — systems don't. Automate transfers to savings, investing, bills the day after payday.
- The system runs while you live. No daily decisions required.
- Set it once, review quarterly. Tinkering is the enemy.

### Conscious Spending
- Spend extravagantly on what you love, cut mercilessly on what you don't.
- Don't follow other people's "frugal" rules. Define your rich life and fund it.
- Big wins live in big categories — housing, transport, food. Don't optimize the latte.

### The Big Wins
- Negotiate salary aggressively — one good raise beats years of penny pinching.
- Pay off high-interest debt first — math beats motivation.
- Index funds, not stock picking. Most active managers underperform; you will too.
- Start now — even small, even imperfect. Time in market beats timing the market.

### Mindset Shifts
- Rich isn't a number, it's a life. Define what yours looks like in detail.
- Stop blaming, start optimizing. Whining doesn't pay bills.
- 85% solution beats 100% paralysis. Get it good enough and move on.

### Practical Setup
- Open separate accounts for fixed costs, savings, investing, guilt-free spending. Buckets prevent mixing.
- Invest in low-cost target-date funds if you don't want to think about allocation.
- Negotiate every recurring bill once a year — phone, internet, insurance. Free money.
- Track net worth monthly, not daily balances. The trend matters; noise doesn't.
