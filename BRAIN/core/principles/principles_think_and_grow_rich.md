## Think and Grow Rich — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Setting goals, building belief, breaking through plateaus, dealing with setbacks.

### Definite Purpose
- A burning desire is the starting point of all achievement. Vague wishes produce nothing.
- Write down exactly what you want, when, and what you'll give in return. Ambiguity kills.
- Read your purpose statement aloud morning and night. Repetition programs the subconscious.

### Faith & Belief
- Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve. Belief is a precondition.
- Repetition of belief becomes conviction. Affirm relentlessly.
- Doubt is the great killer — eliminate it before action.

### Specialized Knowledge & Mastermind
- General knowledge is plentiful and worth little. Specialized knowledge wins.
- Surround yourself with a mastermind — people committed to your goals and theirs. Group mind exceeds individual.
- Don't try to know everything — know who knows what you need.

### Persistence
- Persistence is the difference between failure and success. Most quit before the breakthrough.
- Every adversity carries the seed of equal or greater benefit. Look for it.
- Decision-making is a habit. Decide quickly, change slowly.

### Action Principles
- Plan + action + persistence = result. Missing any one breaks the chain.
- The subconscious works on whatever you feed it. Feed it wins, vivid goals, gratitude.
- Failure is a tester, not a finisher. It only ends the journey if you let it.
- Opportunity often comes disguised as misfortune or temporary defeat.
