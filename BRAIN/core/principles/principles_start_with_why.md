## Start With Why — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Brand messaging, hiring, leadership, building loyal customer base.

### The Golden Circle
- Why → How → What. Most companies communicate from outside in. Great ones go inside out.
- Why is your purpose, cause, or belief. How is your unique approach. What is the product.
- People don't buy what you do, they buy why you do it.

### Inspire vs Manipulate
- Two ways to influence — inspire or manipulate.
- Manipulation works short-term: discounts, fear, peer pressure. It doesn't build loyalty.
- Inspiration works long-term: shared belief, identity, purpose.

### Loyalty Comes from Why
- Customers loyal to your why will follow you across products.
- Apple's customers don't buy laptops — they buy "think different."
- Build the why first, then everything else cascades.

### Hiring on Why
- Hire people who believe what you believe. Skills can be taught — belief can't.
- The right team isn't the most skilled, it's the most aligned.
- A team that shares the why will outperform a team that shares only a paycheck.

### The Celery Test
- When you know your why, every decision becomes obvious. Either it fits the why or it doesn't.
- Without a why, every decision feels political and arbitrary.
- Great brands make decisions easier, not harder, by anchoring to why.
