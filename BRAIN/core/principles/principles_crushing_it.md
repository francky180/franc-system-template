## Crushing It! — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Building personal brand, content strategy, audience building, social media.

### Authenticity Wins
- People can smell fake from a mile away. Be your weird real self.
- Pretending to be someone else exhausts you and bores the audience.
- Your unique voice is your competitive advantage.

### Document, Don't Create
- You don't need to be a content genius. Document what you're already doing.
- Behind-the-scenes is more interesting than polished output.
- Lower the friction to publishing — you'll publish more.

### Be Everywhere Your Audience Is
- The best platform is the one where your audience pays attention.
- Don't pick favorites — meet them where they are.
- Test multiple, double down on what works.

### Patience + Hustle
- Building a brand takes years. Most people quit at month six.
- Hustle hard daily, but be patient with results.
- Compound interest applies to attention, not just money.

### Provide Value First
- Give value 90% of the time, ask 10%. Most do the opposite and wonder why no one buys.
- Free education and entertainment build trust at scale.
- The ask only works after the giving has happened.

### Practical Rules
- Create more than you consume.
- Engage with comments. Conversation is the differentiator.
- Don't optimize for likes. Optimize for the people who become customers.
- Your personal brand is your biggest asset. Build it before you need it.
