## The 10X Rule — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Setting goals, breaking out of mediocrity, sales targets, scaling ambition.

### Set 10x Targets
- Whatever you think you can achieve, multiply it by 10.
- Most goals are too small. Small goals produce small results — and worse, small thinking.
- The discomfort of a 10x goal forces breakthrough action.

### Take 10x Action
- Estimate the action required, then multiply by 10.
- Most failures are not bad strategy — they are too little action.
- Massive action separates winners from everyone else.

### Four Levels of Action
- Do nothing.
- Retreat.
- Take normal action.
- Take massive action.
- Most people live at level 3. Move to level 4.

### Embrace Obsession
- Obsession is a virtue, not a flaw, when directed at the right thing.
- Average people are jealous of obsession because they don't have any.
- Get obsessed about the work, not the recognition.

### Be Unreasonable
- Reasonable goals get reasonable results. The world rewards the unreasonable.
- Don't ask permission. Don't wait for confidence.
- Confidence comes from massive action, not before it.

### Practical Code
- Domination is not a dirty word. Aim to dominate your category.
- Average is a failing formula. Avoid it like a disease.
- Don't reduce targets, increase actions.
- Success is your duty, obligation, and responsibility — not just an option.
