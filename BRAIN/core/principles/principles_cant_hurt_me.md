## Can't Hurt Me — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Facing hard challenges, pushing through quit points, building mental toughness.

### Take Souls
- When someone underestimates you, use their doubt as fuel and crush expectations.
- "Take their soul" — exceed what they believed possible. The shock of your performance compounds in your favor.
- Be the person nobody can predict.

### The 40% Rule
- When your mind says you're done, you're only at 40%. 60% is still in the tank.
- Train yourself to push past the first quit signal. The body lies to protect itself.
- Most of life's limits are mental, not physical.

### Accountability Mirror
- Look at yourself honestly every day. Name your weaknesses out loud.
- Write them on the mirror. See them every morning. No hiding from them.
- Self-deception is the comfort that kills growth.

### Callus Your Mind
- Suffer on purpose to build tolerance for suffering. Cold showers, hard runs, missed meals.
- Voluntary hardship makes involuntary hardship survivable.
- The more discomfort you've trained for, the smaller your problems feel.

### The Cookie Jar
- Keep a mental list of every hard thing you've already overcome.
- When you're breaking, reach into the cookie jar. Remember you've done worse.
- Past wins are fuel for future battles.

### Practical Code
- No one is coming to save you. Save yourself.
- Excuses are for the weak. Own everything — circumstances, mistakes, outcomes.
- The only way out is through. Don't dodge the hard thing — run at it.
