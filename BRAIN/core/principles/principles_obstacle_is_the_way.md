## The Obstacle is the Way — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Setbacks, failures, unfair situations, stuck moments, hard problems.

### Three Disciplines
- Perception — see clearly without emotion or distortion.
- Action — act with focus and persistence on what's in your control.
- Will — accept what cannot be changed, endure what must be endured.

### Perception
- The obstacle is not the enemy. Your reaction is.
- See situations as they are, not as you fear them. Subtract emotion.
- The obstacle in the path becomes the path. Look for the gift inside the problem.

### Action
- Get moving. Action starves doubt.
- Iterate fast. Failure is data, not defeat.
- Persistence beats genius. Most who quit are 90% there.

### Will
- Some things can't be changed. Accept them and move forward.
- Build your inner fortress. The mind is the only place no one can attack you.
- Memento mori. Death gives meaning to action.

### Practical Application
- Reframe every obstacle as an opportunity to prove yourself.
- The harder the test, the bigger the victory.
- The path of least resistance leads to small lives. Take the harder path.
- What stands in the way becomes the way.
