## $100M Leads — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Building lead generation, scaling marketing, designing outreach, choosing channels.

### The Lead Goldmine
- A lead is anyone you can contact. Engaged leads are leads who showed interest. Customers come from engaged leads.
- Volume of leads x quality of offer = revenue. Both must be high.
- Stop trying to convert better. Get more leads first, then optimize.

### The Four Core Methods
- Warm outreach — message people you know. Free, fast, easy to start.
- Post free content — give value publicly. Builds trust at scale.
- Cold outreach — message strangers with relevance. Volume game with right targeting.
- Run paid ads — buy attention. Fastest path to scale once unit economics work.

### Scaling Each
- Once one method works, hire to scale it before adding a second.
- Most failed lead gen is from running 4 methods at 25% effort instead of 1 at 100%.
- Master one, then layer.

### The Lead Magnet
- Solve a narrow problem completely. Not "general info" — a specific result.
- The lead magnet is a sample of the paid product. Show your work.
- Make it so good it could be sold. The fact that it's free makes it irresistible.

### Volume Wins
- More volume = more data = better targeting = better conversion. The flywheel.
- 100 outreaches a day is a strategy. 5 a week is a hobby.
- Activity precedes outcome by weeks. Don't measure conversion when you haven't measured volume yet.
- Hire affiliates, agencies, employees once a method is proven. Force-multiply what works.
