## Jab Jab Jab Right Hook — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Social content, ad creative, platform marketing, sequencing offers.

### Jabs and Right Hooks
- Jabs = giving value. Entertainment, education, inspiration with no ask.
- Right hooks = the ask. The sale, the signup, the conversion.
- You earn the right to the right hook by throwing many jabs first.

### Native to Platform
- A great Instagram post fails on LinkedIn. Each platform has its own language.
- Don't copy-paste across platforms. Translate.
- Spend time consuming the platform before producing for it.

### Quality Over Quantity, But Both
- Great content beats lots of content. But lots of great content beats both.
- Master one platform before adding another.
- Experiment relentlessly until you find what resonates.

### Storytelling First
- Marketing is storytelling. Bad marketers pitch; good marketers narrate.
- Every post is a chapter. The product is part of the story, not the whole story.
- Make the customer the hero, not your brand.

### Practical Rules
- Watch the platform algorithms. They reward different things over time.
- Test the right hook only after testing the jabs work.
- The best brands give 99% and ask 1% — and earn loyalty.
- Pay attention to what people say about you, not just what you say to them.
