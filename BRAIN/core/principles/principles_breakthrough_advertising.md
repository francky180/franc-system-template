## Breakthrough Advertising — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Writing sales copy, ad creatives, landing pages, especially at scale.

### Mass Desire
- You can't create desire. You can only channel existing desire toward your product.
- Find the desire already burning in the market. Tap into it, don't manufacture it.
- The strongest copy connects an existing want to your specific solution.

### Stages of Market Awareness
- Unaware — doesn't know there's a problem. Hard sell. Lead with story or curiosity.
- Problem aware — knows the problem, not the solution. Lead with the problem and agitate.
- Solution aware — knows solutions exist, doesn't know yours. Lead with the solution category.
- Product aware — knows your product. Lead with the offer and price.
- Most aware — ready to buy. Lead with the deal. Match the message to the awareness level.

### Stages of Sophistication
- Stage 1 — virgin market. Simple claim wins.
- Stage 2 — competition. Bigger claim wins.
- Stage 3 — claims exhausted. Mechanism (how) wins.
- Stage 4 — mechanism exhausted. Bigger or new mechanism wins.
- Stage 5 — sophisticated market. Identification with the prospect wins.

### Headlines
- Headlines do 80% of the work. Spend most of your time here.
- A great headline contains a promise, news, curiosity, or all three.
- Specific beats vague. Numbers beat adjectives.

### Practical Power
- Read the prospect, not the product. Know their language, fears, and dreams.
- Channel desire — never argue against it. Riding the wave beats fighting the current.
- The best copy doesn't feel like copy. It feels like a friend telling you something important.
