## Traffic Secrets — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Growing audience, choosing acquisition channels, building distribution.

### Know Your Dream Customer
- Define exactly who you're trying to reach — age, income, beliefs, fears, where they hang out.
- The clearer the target, the easier the channel choice.
- Don't market to everyone. Marketing to everyone reaches no one.

### Find Where They Already Are
- Your dream customers are congregating somewhere right now. Find the watering holes.
- Search for the influencers, podcasts, forums, subreddits where they live.
- Don't build a new channel. Hijack existing attention.

### The Dream 100
- Identify the top 100 places your audience gathers. Influencers, publications, communities.
- Build relationships with each over time. One Dream 100 placement beats 1000 cold ads.
- Give before asking. Add value to their world for months before pitching.

### Hook, Story, Offer (Channel Version)
- Each channel demands its own hook style. Same message, different bait.
- Test hooks at low cost before scaling. If a hook flops, the channel isn't the problem.
- Match the channel — long form on YouTube, short on TikTok, professional on LinkedIn.

### Own the Traffic
- Convert every interaction into an email subscriber. Channels die; lists don't.
- Diversify across multiple traffic sources. One algorithm change shouldn't kill you.
- The ultimate edge: distribution. Whoever owns the audience wins.
