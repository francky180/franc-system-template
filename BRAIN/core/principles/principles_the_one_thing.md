## The ONE Thing — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Prioritizing daily, choosing where to focus, eliminating busywork.

### The Focusing Question
- "What's the ONE thing I can do, such that by doing it, everything else becomes easier or unnecessary?"
- Ask it daily, weekly, quarterly, yearly.
- The question forces you out of busywork into impact.

### Domino Effect
- Big results come from a sequence of small wins, each making the next bigger.
- One domino can knock down another 50% larger than itself.
- Stack the dominoes deliberately.

### Lies That Stop You
- Everything matters equally — no, 80/20 rules.
- Multitasking — no, it kills depth and quality.
- A disciplined life — no, you only need disciplined habits, not discipline everywhere.
- Willpower is on call — no, it depletes. Use it on the ONE thing first.

### Time Block the ONE Thing
- Block time for your most important task before anything else.
- Defend it. No meetings, no email, no interruptions.
- 4 hours of focused work beats 12 hours of fragmented work.

### Practical Application
- Start every day asking the focusing question.
- Most people fail by spreading effort. Concentrated effort beats scattered effort 10x.
- Going small means ignoring the many to focus on the one.
- Extraordinary results require focused attention. There is no shortcut.
