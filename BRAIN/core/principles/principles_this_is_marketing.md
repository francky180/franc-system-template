## This Is Marketing — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Building a brand, choosing who to serve, designing offers, marketing ethically.

### Find the Smallest Viable Audience
- Don't try to reach everyone. Find the smallest group of people whose lives you can change.
- Serve them deeply. They will tell others.
- Mass market is a trap. Specific is scalable.

### People Like Us Do Things Like This
- The most powerful marketing message in the world.
- Identity > information. People act based on who they think they are, not what they're told.
- Build a tribe with a shared identity, then offer products that affirm it.

### Marketing is Change
- Marketing is the act of making change happen. If you're not trying to change anyone, you're not marketing.
- Decide who you want to change, and into what.
- Be specific about the before and after states.

### Empathy First
- See the world through your customer's eyes. Their dreams, fears, biases.
- Tell stories that resonate with their existing worldview.
- Don't try to teach people new worldviews — meet them in theirs.

### Permission Marketing
- Earn the right to message someone. Spam is the opposite of marketing.
- The privilege of an open inbox is precious. Don't waste it.
- Anticipated, personal, relevant messages get through. Everything else gets deleted.

### Practical Rules
- Marketing helps people get what they already want.
- Tension is not a bug. The best marketing creates productive tension.
- Don't seek to be average for average people. Find the people who care, and care more.
- Make things people want to talk about. Then make it easy to talk about them.
