## Antifragile — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Designing businesses, personal habits, investment portfolios, anything that must survive shocks.

### Three States
- Fragile — breaks under stress. Glass.
- Robust — survives stress unchanged. Rock.
- Antifragile — improves under stress. Muscle, immune system, evolution.
- Aim for antifragile, not just robust.

### Optionality
- Have options without obligations. Maximum upside, capped downside.
- The man with options is rich, even with no money.
- In business: many small experiments beat one big bet.

### Via Negativa
- Subtraction beats addition. The strongest improvements come from removing harms, not adding helps.
- Cut bad food before adding supplements. Cut bad clients before adding new ones. Cut waste before adding revenue.
- "What should I stop doing" is more powerful than "what should I start doing."

### Skin in the Game
- People should bear the consequences of their advice. Without skin in the game, advice is cheap and dangerous.
- Don't trust experts who have nothing to lose if they're wrong.
- Put your own skin in the game — it focuses you and signals trust to others.

### Practical Antifragility
- Multiple income streams beat one big salary. The big salary is fragile.
- Stress your body deliberately — fasting, cold, exercise — to make it stronger.
- Welcome volatility in inputs that make you adapt; reject volatility in catastrophic outputs.
- The barbell strategy — extreme safety on one end, extreme risk on the other, nothing in the middle.
