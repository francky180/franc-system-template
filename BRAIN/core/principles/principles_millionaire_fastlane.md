## The Millionaire Fastlane — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Choosing what business to build, evaluating scale potential, escaping the slow lane.

### Three Wealth Roads
- Sidewalk — no plan, paycheck to paycheck, lottery dreams. Avoid.
- Slowlane — save 10% for 40 years, retire old. Trades time for tiny wealth.
- Fastlane — build a business that generates cash flow now and scales. Time is the asset.

### CENTS Framework (qualifying a business)
- Control — you own the platform, not rent it from Amazon/FB. Renters get evicted.
- Entry — high barrier to entry = less competition. If anyone can do it, returns die.
- Need — solve a real problem. Selfish ideas fail; people-serving ideas scale.
- Time — works without your hands on it. If you stop, income stops, it's a job.
- Scale — can reach millions. Local pizza shop fails this; SaaS passes.

### Mindset Rules
- Wealth isn't an event, it's a process. The number isn't the goal — the system is.
- Time is the only non-renewable resource. Money you can always make again.
- Stop looking for "passion" — look for problems you can solve at scale.

### Operating Principles
- Solve other people's problems and money follows. Selfishness is the slow lane.
- The road to wealth runs through many. Reach is multiplication.
- Process over event — daily execution beats searching for the one big break.
- Make decisions by consequence, not convenience. Convenience compounds against you.
