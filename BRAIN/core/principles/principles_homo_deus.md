## Homo Deus — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Long-term planning, AI strategy, evaluating future trends, positioning for the next decade.

### From Survival to Optimization
- Humanity has solved famine, plague, and war at unprecedented levels. The new agenda is happiness, immortality, and divinity.
- Where there's an agenda, there's a market. Where there's a market, there's opportunity.

### Data is the New Religion
- Dataism — the belief that the universe consists of data flows and value comes from data processing.
- The most powerful organizations now are data processors, not asset owners.
- Your business should generate, capture, and learn from data by default.

### Algorithms Beating Intuition
- In more and more domains, algorithms beat human judgment.
- Don't fight this trend — use it. Build with AI in the loop or compete against those who do.
- Human "intuition" is itself a pattern recognizer. Algorithms are catching up.

### The Useless Class
- Mass automation may create a class of people whose labor is no longer needed.
- Position yourself in work that combines creativity, empathy, and judgment — the last things to be automated.
- Or own the automation rather than compete with it.

### Practical Implications
- Watch which decisions you're delegating to algorithms. Each one removes a piece of your agency.
- Privacy is the new oil — and like oil, most people will trade it cheaply.
- Build for a world where attention is the scarcest resource.
- The future belongs to those who upgrade themselves continuously, not those who hold ground.
