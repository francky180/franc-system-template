## The Power of Now — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Overthinking, anxiety, racing mind, lack of focus, emotional reactivity.

### Now is All There Is
- The past exists only as memory. The future exists only as anticipation. Both happen in the present.
- The only moment you can act, feel, decide, or be alive is now.
- Stop waiting to live until conditions improve. Live now or never.

### You Are Not Your Mind
- The mind is a tool, not your identity. The constant thinking is not you.
- Watch the thoughts arise without identifying with them.
- The space behind the thoughts is who you really are.

### The Pain-Body
- The accumulated emotional pain you carry has its own life. It feeds on more pain.
- Recognize when it's active. Don't let it run your behavior.
- Awareness alone weakens it.

### Surrender to What Is
- Resistance to the present moment is the source of suffering.
- "Surrender" doesn't mean giving up — it means accepting reality and acting from clarity.
- Fight the situation if needed, not the fact that the situation exists.

### Practical Application
- Notice the next breath you take. That's it — you're present.
- When the mind spirals, return to body sensations. The body is always now.
- Don't postpone joy. There is no later. There is only now.
- Most problems are mental fictions about past or future. Drop them, and the problem often vanishes.
