## Expert Secrets — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Building authority, creating an audience, launching info products, becoming the go-to person in a niche.

### The Charismatic Leader
- People follow people, not companies. Build a personal brand around a clear mission.
- Have an opinion. Strong takes attract followers; neutrality attracts no one.
- Vulnerability is connection. Share your origin story including the failure.

### The New Opportunity
- Don't sell improvement — sell a new opportunity. People resist "better"; they buy "different."
- Position your offer as the missing piece they didn't know existed.
- Frame: "I used to think X. Then I discovered Y. Now everything is different."

### The Movement
- Create a future-based cause your audience can join. Not just a product — a tribe.
- Name your movement, your method, your enemy. People rally around shared identity.
- Build rituals, vocabulary, insider knowledge. Cult-like in the good sense.

### The Epiphany Bridge
- Stories sell better than logic. Walk the prospect through your discovery moment.
- Format: backstory → wall hit → discovery → transformation → invitation.
- Logic justifies what the story sold.

### Stack & Close
- Stack the value of every component until the price feels absurd by comparison.
- Use risk reversal — money-back, results-based, you-do-the-work guarantees.
- Scarcity and urgency are the closers. Make taking action now the only logical move.
- The expert who moves people is the expert who builds wealth.
