## Sapiens — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Understanding markets, brand building, narrative design, long-term trends.

### Stories Run the World
- Humans cooperate at scale because we believe in shared fictions — money, nations, corporations, gods.
- These fictions only work as long as people believe them. Brands work the same way.
- The strongest businesses sell stories more than products.

### Cognitive Revolution
- Language let us gossip and discuss things that don't exist. Both are critical.
- The ability to invent shared reality is humanity's superpower. Use it deliberately.
- Build a story your customers can tell themselves.

### Money is the Greatest Story
- Money is a system of mutual trust. It only has value because everyone agrees it does.
- The same principle applies to brands. Trust is the underlying asset.
- Lose trust and the value evaporates instantly.

### Empires and Scale
- Empires expand by absorbing diverse cultures into one shared story.
- Successful companies do the same — onboard disparate users into a unified culture.
- Standardization beats local variation at scale.

### Modern Lessons
- Capitalism is built on credit — the belief that the future will be bigger than the present.
- Science accelerated by admitting ignorance. Stop pretending you know everything.
- Happiness doesn't track with progress. More wealth ≠ more meaning. Watch for the trap.
- The biggest bets — agriculture, religion, capitalism — were not chosen. They emerged. Choose yours deliberately.
