## Discipline Equals Freedom — Key Principles

**When to apply:** Building habits, breaking through resistance, daily execution.

### Discipline = Freedom
- Discipline isn't restriction. It's the path to freedom.
- The disciplined have time, money, energy, and choices. The undisciplined have none.
- Pay the price now or pay much more later.

### Get Up Early
- The day is won before everyone else wakes up.
- Pre-dawn hours are uncontaminated by other people's needs.
- Use them for the most important work.

### No Excuses
- Don't let yourself negotiate. The brain will always find reasons to skip.
- The moment you make exceptions, the system collapses.
- Excuses are luxuries the disciplined don't afford.

### Default Aggressive
- When in doubt, attack the problem. Hesitation kills momentum.
- Move toward fear, not away from it.
- The aggressive default beats waiting for perfect conditions.

### Small Wins Stack
- Make the bed. Hit the workout. Eat the protein. Each small win sets up the next.
- Discipline compounds — and so does its absence.
- One missed day rarely matters. The pattern of missed days does.

### Practical Code
- The only way out is through. Stop looking for shortcuts.
- Mental toughness is built daily, not in big moments.
- Stay humble. Stay hungry. Stay disciplined.
- Good. Whatever happens, the answer is "good." It means there's something to learn.
