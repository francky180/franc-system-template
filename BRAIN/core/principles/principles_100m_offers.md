## $100M Offers — Core Principles

**When to apply:** Creating service offers, pricing, writing landing pages, designing funnels, packaging products for Credit repair, Trading, Agency.

### The Grand Slam Offer Framework
A Grand Slam Offer is so good people feel stupid saying no.

### The Value Equation
**Value = (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay x Effort & Sacrifice)**

- **Increase dream outcome** — Make the result bigger. "Fix your credit" < "Get approved for your dream home."
- **Increase perceived likelihood** — Social proof, guarantees, case studies. Make them believe it works.
- **Decrease time delay** — Faster results = more valuable. "90 days" < "30 days" < "instant."
- **Decrease effort/sacrifice** — Done-for-you > done-with-you > do-it-yourself. Remove friction.

### Creating Irresistible Offers
1. **Identify the dream outcome** — What does the customer actually want? Not credit repair — they want approval, a house, freedom.
2. **List every problem** — What stops them from getting there? List every obstacle, fear, concern.
3. **Create solutions for each problem** — Each solution becomes a component of your offer.
4. **Stack the value** — Bundle solutions so the total perceived value far exceeds the price.
5. **Add bonuses** — Free tools, templates, guides that increase perceived value.
6. **Add a guarantee** — Remove risk entirely. "90-day money-back" or "we work free until it's done."
7. **Create urgency and scarcity** — Limited spots, deadline, cohort-based. Must be real.
8. **Name it** — Give your offer a unique name. "Credit Repair Pro" not "credit repair service."

### Pricing Principles
- **Price on value, not cost** — What is the result worth to them? Price relative to that.
- **Higher prices = better clients** — People who pay more, pay attention. Cheap clients complain most.
- **The goal is to be so good they can't ignore you** — Not the cheapest. The most valuable.
- **Commoditization is the enemy** — If your offer sounds like everyone else's, you compete on price. Differentiate.

### Applied to Franc's Businesses
- **Credit repair:** The dream is not "clean credit report" — it's "get approved for your house/car/apartment." Frame the offer around the outcome.
- **Trading:** The dream is not "learn forex" — it's "financial freedom, quit your job, trade from anywhere." Sell the lifestyle.
- **Agency:** The dream is not "get a website" — it's "get clients while you sleep, automate your business." Sell the system.
